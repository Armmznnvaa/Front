Homemade Apple Pie Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 6-8 medium-sized apples(peeled,cored,and sliced)
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teapoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons butter (cut into small pieces)
  • 2 unbaked pie crusts(Homemade or store-bought)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425F (220C)
  2. In a large mixing bowl,combine the sliced apples,sugar,flour,cinnamon,and salt.Toss until the apples are evely coated.
  3. Place one of the pie crusts into a 9-inchpie dish.
  4. Pour one of apple mixture into the pie crust,spreading it out evenly.
  5. Dot the top of the apple mixeture with small pieces of butter.
  6. Place the second pie crust over the apples and crims the edges to seal the pie.
  7. Using A sharp knife,make a few samll slist in the top crust to aloow steam to escape.
  8. Bake The pie in the preheated oven for 45-55 mintues or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbling.
  9. remove the pie from the oven and allow it to cool for at least 30 minytes before serving.

Serving Suggestion:

Serve your homemade apple pie warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream for a delicious treat!

Enjoy!
